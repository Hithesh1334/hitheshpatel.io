The short version

Most of my work starts the same way: a data system that's broken or brittle, and needs to be rebuilt to be scalable, modular, and maintainable. That rebuild is my job.

For 2+ years at phData, I've modernized financial data ecosystems for enterprise clients: building secure, production-grade pipelines on dbt, Snowflake, and Airflow.

Lately I've been wiring LLM-powered agents and custom MCP tooling into our engineering workflows, collapsing multi-day investigations into a single prompt, without handing the keys to a black box.

I care about clean, maintainable code, proactive communication, and taking full ownership of what I ship.
